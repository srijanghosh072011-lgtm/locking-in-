We know Regina's housing stock

Old or new, we've plumbed one like yours

Regina is a city of very different houses, and each comes with its own plumbing quirks. We work on all of them, every week:

  • Cathedral, Transitional Area & the Crescents — older homes with clay sewers, tree-root backups and tired galvanized supply lines.
  • Lakeview & Hillsdale — mid-century bungalows due for repipes and water-heater upgrades.
  • Harbour Landing, The Greens & Eastbrook — newer builds where we handle fixtures, tankless and finishing basements.
  • Downtown & Warehouse District — light-commercial and condo plumbing.
