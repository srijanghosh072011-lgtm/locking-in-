Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 1 November 2025

Wascana Plumbing & Drain is committed to making our website usable for everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

What we've done

  • Semantic, keyboard-friendly markup. The whole site can be navigated with a keyboard, with a visible focus indicator and a "skip to content" link.
  • Readable contrast. Text and interactive elements are designed to meet or exceed the 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text.
  • Descriptive text alternatives. Meaningful images include alt text, and icon-only controls have accessible labels.
  • Clear forms. Every field has a visible label, helpful error messages, and validation that's announced to assistive technology.
  • Respect for motion preferences. Animations are minimal and are reduced automatically if your device requests reduced motion.
  • Responsive design. The site reflows cleanly to any screen size without loss of content or horizontal scrolling.

Ongoing work

Accessibility is never "finished." We test with keyboard navigation and screen readers, and we review new content as it's added. If you use assistive technology and something doesn't work the way it should, we want to know.
