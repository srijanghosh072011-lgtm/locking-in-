Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 November 2025

Wascana Plumbing & Drain Ltd. ("Wascana", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

What we collect

  • Information you give us — your name, phone number, email, service address and the details of your plumbing request when you book, request a quote, or contact us.
  • Service records — work performed, invoices, warranty information and notes needed to service your home.
  • Site usage — basic, anonymized analytics (pages viewed, device type) only after you accept cookies. See "Cookies" below.

Why we collect it

  • To schedule, perform and warranty your plumbing service.
  • To contact you about your request, booking or invoice.
  • To send seasonal tips and offers — only if you expressly opt in (CASL consent). Every such email includes a one-click unsubscribe.
  • To improve our website and services.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to anyone, ever.

Who we share it with

We share information only with service providers who help us operate — for example, our booking and scheduling software, payment processor and email provider — and only the minimum they need. These providers are bound by contract to protect your information and use it only on our instructions. We may also disclose information if required by law.

How we protect it

This website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Form submissions are validated and filtered for spam and malicious input. Access to customer records is limited to staff who need it, and our systems are protected with strong authentication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable, industry-standard measures to safeguard your information.

How long we keep it

We retain customer and service records only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal, tax and warranty obligations — typically up to seven years for financial records. Marketing contact information is kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it. When information is no longer needed, we securely destroy it.

Cookies & analytics

Essential cookies keep the site working. We do not load analytics or any non-essential cookies until you choose "Accept" on our cookie banner. You can decline, and you can clear your choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data. We do not use advertising trackers.

Your rights

You may ask to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you may withdraw consent (including marketing consent) at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by PIPEDA.

Children

Our services and website are directed to adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be posted on this page.
