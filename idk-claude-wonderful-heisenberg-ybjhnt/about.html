Our story

One van, one promise

Wascana Plumbing & Drain started in 2009 when Dave Kowalchuk — a journeyperson plumber tired of watching homeowners get nickel-and-dimed — bought a used van and hung out his own shingle. The promise was simple: tell people the price before you start, do the job right, and clean up after yourself.

Turns out that's rarer than it should be. Word got around the Crescents, then Lakeview, then the whole city. Today the family runs a fleet of fully-stocked trucks and a team of licensed plumbers — but every single one of them works to that same promise. The name comes from Wascana, the creek and lake at the heart of Regina. We figured a local landmark beat another "Mr." or "Pro" anything.
