Signs your drain is telling you something

The warnings most people ignore

  • Sinks or tubs draining slower than they used to
  • Gurgling toilets, or one fixture backing up when you run another
  • A bad smell from a floor drain or the yard cleanout
  • Recurring backups every few months — a classic sign of tree roots in the main

In Regina's older neighbourhoods like Cathedral, Transitional Area and the Crescents, mature elms and clay sewer pipe are a notorious combination. We see it every week — and we fix it for good rather than just buying you time.

How we clear it

The right tool for the blockage

Cable snaking

For soft clogs and hair/grease in branch lines. Fast, affordable, and gets most kitchen and bathroom drains flowing again on the spot.

Hydro-jetting

High-pressure water scours the full pipe diameter — grease, scale and roots. The closest thing to a brand-new line without digging.

Camera inspection

We send a camera down the line and show you the screen. You see the actual problem and only pay to fix what's really there.
