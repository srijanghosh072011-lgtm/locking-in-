Our story

Local plumbers who actually pick up the phone

BlueLine started in a single van in 2009 with one rule: treat every home like it’s your mum’s. Fifteen years and 12,000 jobs later, that hasn’t changed.

Why we exist

We got tired of bad tradie experiences

Late arrivals. Vague “we’ll see when we get there” pricing. Muddy boots on the carpet. We built BlueLine to be the opposite of all that — a plumbing company that respects your time, your home and your budget.

Today we’re a team of fully licensed plumbers and gas fitters covering Greater Vancouver, but we still run on the same principles we started with: turn up when we say, quote before we start, and leave the place cleaner than we found it.
