Privacy Policy

BlueLine Plumbing Ltd. (“BlueLine”, “we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights under Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial law.

Information we collect

  • Contact & booking details — name, phone, email, service address, and the details of the work you request.
  • Payment information — processed by our PCI-compliant payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Account & service history — invoices, warranty records and past jobs, if you have a customer account.
  • Technical data — with your consent, cookies and analytics that tell us how the site is used (see “Cookies”).

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries, provide quotes and carry out plumbing services.
  • To schedule jobs, send confirmations and reminders, and issue invoices and warranties.
  • To improve our services and website.
  • With your express consent, to send seasonal tips and offers by email (CASL — see below).

Consent & marketing (CASL)

We only send commercial electronic messages with your express or implied consent, as required by Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). Every marketing email identifies us, includes our mailing address, and provides a working unsubscribe link that we action promptly. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to make the site work. Only with your consent (via our cookie banner) do we set analytics cookies. You can change your choice anytime by clearing the consent stored in your browser, and you can block cookies in your browser settings.

Sharing your information

We don’t sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate — for example payment processing, scheduling/CRM, email delivery and hosting — under agreements that require them to protect it and use it only for those services.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal, tax and warranty obligations, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

Security

We protect your data with HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit, access controls, hashed passwords, and reputable hosting with backups. No method is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you, and ask questions about how it’s handled. Contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@bluelineplumbing.ca. If you’re not satisfied, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above shows the current version.
