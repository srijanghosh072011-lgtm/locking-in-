Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 1 June 2026

BlueLine Plumbing is committed to making our website usable for everyone, including people who rely on assistive technologies.

Our standard

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, consistent with the goals of the Accessible Canada Act and provincial accessibility legislation.

What we’ve done

  • Semantic HTML with logical heading structure and landmark regions.
  • Text and interactive elements meet 4.5:1 colour-contrast minimums.
  • Full keyboard navigation with a visible focus indicator and a “skip to content” link.
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images and labels on every form field.
  • Touch targets of at least 44×44px and a responsive layout down to small screens.
  • Respect for the “reduced motion” system setting.

Known limitations

Some third-party content (such as embedded maps or review widgets) may not fully meet our standard. We choose accessible providers where possible and are happy to provide the information another way.

Feedback

If you encounter any barrier on our site, please tell us — we’ll help directly and fix the issue. Email access@bluelineplumbing.ca or call (604) 555-0188, and we’ll respond within two business days.
